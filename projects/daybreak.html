Web Design

Daybreak

Overview

Figma Static Site Github

Daybreak is a K-pop Dance Crew at SCU that unites people through the love of K-pop and dance. As an active board member of Daybreak, I wanted to explore a potential website for our club that could serve as a digital hub for the club members while promoting our clubs to the world.

Objective: Create a potential website for Daybreak to promote the club and provide useful information to the club members.

Toolkit: Figma, InDesign

Programming Languages: HTML, CSS, JS

The target audience includes potential and current club members, as well as external dance groups, primarily individuals aged 17-25. Typical activities includes subscribing to our newsletter, checking upcoming events, and registering for dance covers.
