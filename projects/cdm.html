Web Design

Children's Discovery Museum

Skills

UI Design

Wireframes

Toolkit

Figma

Duration

2 Months (2023)

Overview

View in Figma

As a nonprofit interactive museum, the Children’s Discovery Museum fosters creativity and curiosity in youth through hands-on exhibits. Having cherished many joyful memories from my childhood visits, I was inspired to redesign the museum’s outdated website.

Problem Statement

The current website for the Children's Discovery Museum fails to effectively engage its primary audiences—parents with young children, schools planning field trips, and young children themselves. The design is outdated and does not capture the excitement and creativity of the museum's interactive exhibits. This results in a less inviting online presence, limiting the museum's ability to attract and engage visitors.

Goal

The goal of the redesign is to create a vibrant and engaging website that accurately reflects the museum's interactive environment. The new design will enhance usability, capture the playful spirit of the museum, and maintain the museum's branding.

Target Audiences

  • Parents with young children
  • Young children
  • Local schools planning field trips

Persona

To better understand the target audience and their needs, I created personas representing typical users.

Typical Tasks

By developing the persona, I identified the most common tasks users typically perform when visiting the website.

  • Purchasing Tickets and Membership
  • Browsing Exhibitions
  • Learning About the Museum

Site Structure

Before starting the redesign, I analyzed the site structure and identified the key content that needed to be included.

Looking at this overwhelming diagram, we can emphasize the challenges users face in navigating to relevant pages. For my redesign, I carefully reviewed all the pages and prioritized the most essential ones, focusing on the Home page, Visit page, Ticket page, and About page.

Mood Board

Next, I created a mood board that captures a modern and playful feel, while incorporating elements from the museum's original branding.

Wireframes

Lastly, using Illustrator, I experimented with different page layouts before starting the redesign.

Color Palette

For the final redesign, I chose a color palette that preserves the core brand colors while updating them for a more modern and playful look.

Final Design Walkthrough

Homepage

The homepage features a clean and unique navigation design that makes it easy for visitors to find what they need. I’ve also incorporated playful duck footprints, inspired by Discovery Duck—the museum's mascot—to add a touch of liveliness to the website.

Visit Page

Quickly view ongoing exhibits and museum hours to start planning a visit! Lively duck footprints can be seen throughout the site to guide the users around the page.

Ticket Page

A clean ticket page with clear pricing, easy for parents and children to understand.

About Page

Discover the museum's mission, meet the staff, and support the museum through the About Page.
