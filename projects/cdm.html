Web Design

Children Discovery Museum

Skills

UI Design

Toolkit

Figma

Duration

2 Months (2023)

Overview

View in Figma

As a nonprofit interactive museum, the Children’s Discovery Museum fosters creativity and curiosity in youth through hands-on exhibits. Having cherished many joyful memories from my childhood visits, I was inspired to redesign the museum’s website after observing its design and technical issues. My goal was to capture the excitement and playful atmosphere of the museum, reflecting the engaging experience I once had as a visitor.

Problem Statement

The current website for the Children's Discovery Museum fails to effectively engage its primary audiences—parents with young children, schools planning field trips, and young children themselves. The design is outdated and does not capture the excitement and creativity of the museum's interactive exhibits. This results in a less inviting online presence, limiting the museum's ability to attract and engage visitors.

Goal

The goal of the redesign is to create a vibrant and engaging website that accurately reflects the museum's interactive environment. The new design will enhance usability, capture the playful spirit of the museum, and maintain the museum's branding.

Target Audiences

  • Parents with young children
  • Young children
  • Schools planning field trips

Site Structure

Before starting the redesign, I analyzed the site structure and identified the key content that needed to be included. This foundational research was crucial in ensuring that the redesigned site would effectively meet the museum's needs and deliver an enhanced user experience.

Mood Board

The mood board captures a modern and playful feel while incorporating colors and elements from the museum's original branding.

Colors

The redesign features a color palette that retains the overall brand colors but is adjusted to give it a more modern feel.

Final Design

Homepage

The homepage features a clean and unique navigation design that makes it easy for visitors to find what they need. I’ve also incorporated playful duck footprints, inspired by the museum’s mascot, to add a touch of liveliness to the website.

Vist Page

Quickly view ongoing exhibits and museum hours to start planning a visit! Lively duck footprints can be seen throughout the site to guide the users around the page.

Ticket Page

A clean and visual ticket page that clearly displays prices, making it easy for both parents and children to understand.

About Page

Discover the museum's mission, meet the staff, and support the museum through donations.
