Overview

Cakery is a logo and brand design project inspired by one of my favorite animalsâ€”bunnies. The goal of the project is to create a logo and brand identity centered around the charm and cuteness of bunnies.

The final design features an adorable logo that combines a cute bunny with a birthday cake. The elements in the logo are tweaked and integrated into various packaging designs to bring playfulness and joy to every aspect of the bakery. The soft color palette is carefully selected to evoke joy and create a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Foundation Work

Before beginning the design process, I researched rabbits to better understand their characteristics and physical appearance. This research allowed me to incorporate them effectively and creatively into the logo and brand design.

Research Report

Logo Sketches

Through sketching, I explored a range of logo styles and brand concepts. Ultimately, I chose to further develop Logo 12, as I loved how an upside-down bunny creatively resembles frosting drips on cakes.

Mood Board

After finalizing the rough logo, I began developing a brand around the idea of a local bakery. I envisioned myself as a bakery owner, crafting a brand that would be both joyful and welcoming for everyone in the community. With this vision in mind, I created the mood board below:

Logo Iterations

Below are two of the iterations created in Illustrator. From the left logo to the right, I added horizontal bars to represent layers of the cake. This improved the readability of the logo as a cake.

Final Logo Design

With more refinement and small changes, the final logo design was created! I swapped out the original circle with a flame to better represent a birthday cake and to evoke a sense of celebration and joy.

The colors, soft pink and soft brown, were chosen to evoke a sense of love, playfulness, and warmth.

Package Design

After finalizing the logo, I tackled the package design by sketching a variety of potential packaging and merchandise for the brand. Using pencil and paper allowed me to explore limitless possibilities for the mockups.

Final Mockups

The final mockup showcases a variety of packaging that Cakery would use. Some designs feature the entire logo, while others incorporate elements of the logo, molding to the shape of each specific packaging type.
