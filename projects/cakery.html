Overview

View in Figma

Cakery is a logo and brand design project created through the prompt of Design an animal logo and develop a complete brand identity centered around this logo.

The final design features an adorable logo that combines a cute bunny with a cake, making it unique and memorable. The elements in the logo are tweaked and integrated into various packaging designs to bring playfulness to every aspect of the bakery. The soft color palette and design details are carefully selected to evoke joy and create a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Initial Appraoch

Due to the broad scope of the design prompt, I began by choosing an adorable bunny, one of my favorite animals at the time. I then researched rabbits to understand their characteristics and explored various shops, apps, and companies to determine how best to incorporate the bunny into the logo design.

Research Report

Logo Sketches

After sketching a variety of logos, I decided to further develop logo number 12. I believed that the combination of the bakery theme with the bunny was a perfect fit and that this design had great potential.

Generating Brand Concept

Once the rough logo was confirmed, I began developing a brand around the idea of a local bakery. I envisioned myself as a bakery owner, crafting a brand that would be joyful and welcoming for everyone in the community. With this vision in mind, I created a mood board and then proceeded to finalize the brand's logo.

Mood Board

Final Logo Design

Through more sketches and bringing the design to Adobe Illustrator, the final logo design was created! I incoperated a birthday cake specfically as it is symbol of celebration and joy. Through additional sketches and refinement in Adobe Illustrator, the final logo design was created!

I incorporated a birthday cake specifically as it symbolizes celebration and joy. The colors, soft pink and soft brown, were chosen to evoke a sense of love, playfulness, and warmth. The brown accent also seamlessly blends with the bakery's baked goods.

Next – Package Design

After finalizing the logo, I tackled the package design by sketching a variety of potential packaging and merchandise for the brand. Using pencil and paper allowed me to explore limitless possibilities for the mockups.

Final Mockup Poster

The final mockup showcases a variety of packaging that Cakery would use, including bakery boxes, cups, bags, and aprons. Some designs feature the entire logo, while others incorporate elements of the logo, molding to the shape of each specific packaging type. The goal of the design is to create a cohesive and identifiable set of packaging that reinforces the brand’s identity and enhances the customer experience.
